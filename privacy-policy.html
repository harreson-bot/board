Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Overview

At Affordable Healthcare Solutions ("we," "us," "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone, mailing address)
  • Information submitted through contact forms on our website
  • Information provided during consultations or calls
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Help you understand your insurance options and provide personalized guidance
  • Send you information about available insurance plans and services

SMS/Text Messaging Consent

By submitting your phone number or contacting us, you consent to receive text messages and calls regarding insurance options and services. Reply "STOP" to any text message to opt out of text communications. This complies with TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act) regulations.

Our website uses basic analytics to understand how visitors use our site. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is used solely to provide insurance guidance and related services.

Our services are not designed for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.
